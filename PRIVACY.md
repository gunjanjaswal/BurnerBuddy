# 🔥 BurnerBuddy Privacy Policy

**Last Updated:** October 28, 2025

---

## 🛡️ Our Commitment to Your Privacy

**BurnerBuddy does NOT collect, store, transmit, or share any user data.**

Your privacy is our top priority. This extension operates entirely on your device with zero data collection.

---

## ✅ What BurnerBuddy Does

- **Generates temporary data** - Creates fake personal information (names, emails, passwords) for form filling
- **Stores locally** - Saves generated data in your browser using Chrome's storage API
- **Simulates emails** - Creates confirmation emails locally on your device
- **Stays on your device** - All data remains on your device only, never transmitted anywhere

---

## ❌ What BurnerBuddy Does NOT Do

We want to be crystal clear about what we don't do:

- ❌ **Does not collect** your real personal information
- ❌ **Does not track** your browsing history
- ❌ **Does not monitor** your activity or behavior
- ❌ **Does not transmit** data to external servers
- ❌ **Does not use** analytics or tracking tools
- ❌ **Does not share** data with third parties
- ❌ **Does not sell** your information (we don't have any to sell!)

---

## 💾 Local Storage

All temporary accounts and simulated emails are stored locally in your browser using Chrome's storage API. 

**This data:**
- ✅ Stays on your device
- ✅ Is never transmitted anywhere
- ✅ Can be deleted at any time by uninstalling the extension
- ✅ Is not accessible to any external services

---

## 🔑 Permissions Explained

BurnerBuddy requests the following permissions to function:

| Permission | Why We Need It |
|------------|----------------|
| **contextMenus** | To add "Fill with BurnerBuddy" to your right-click menu |
| **activeTab** | To fill forms on the current page when you activate it |
| **storage** | To save temporary accounts locally on your device |
| **scripting** | To inject form-filling code when you activate it |

**Important:** These permissions only allow the extension to work when YOU activate it. We never use them to collect data.

---

## 🌐 Third-Party Services

**BurnerBuddy does not use any third-party services, analytics, or external APIs.**

Everything runs locally on your device. No data ever leaves your browser.
